About EmpirePicks

EmpirePicks is an odds intelligence platform. We analyze sportsbook pricing to estimate consensus probabilities, reveal market lean, and highlight potential value signals. We do not guarantee outcomes.

What EmpirePicks is

  • Market interpretation, using sportsbook prices as data.
  • Consensus probability, after removing margin and weighting sharper books.
  • Value signals, showing when a price diverges from consensus.

What EmpirePicks is not

  • Not betting advice, not financial advice.
  • Not a guarantee of profit or outcomes.
  • Not “locks” or “sure things”.

Key terms

Market Implied Probability

The probability suggested by a specific sportsbook’s odds. This includes the sportsbook’s margin. On the game page, “Books favor” reflects this, it is the market’s current lean based on price.

Consensus Probability

An estimate of the “fair” probability after removing margin (no-vig) and aggregating across books, with sharper books weighted more heavily.

Value Signal

A relative indicator showing how favorable a price is versus consensus for that market, adjusted for stability. It is not a prediction of the outcome, it is a pricing signal.

Consensus Stability

A measure of how tightly sportsbooks agree. If books disagree widely, stability is lower, and value signals are dampened.

Sharp vs Public Books

Some books tend to be more efficient in pricing, and are weighted more heavily in consensus. This reduces “public bias” from dominating the aggregate.

How to read a game

  • Market Lean: which side the books are currently favoring (by implied probability).
  • Best Value: which option shows the strongest value signal versus consensus.
  • Consensus: whether books are aligned (higher stability) or dispersed (lower stability).
